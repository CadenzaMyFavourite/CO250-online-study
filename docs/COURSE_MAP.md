# Course map

The actual supplied review sequence defines the course map. Review 2 is the W26 IP/NLP formulation review; Reviews 1 and 3–11 provide the remaining numbered units.

1. **LP formulation** — workflow, decision variables, objectives, constraints, multiperiod models
2. **Integer & nonlinear formulation** — binary variables, logical links, integer and nonlinear models
3. **LP outcomes & certificates** — optimal, infeasible, unbounded; verification vectors
4. **Standard equality form** — conversion, bases, basic solutions, BFSs, canonical form
5. **Simplex algorithm** — entering/leaving variables, ratio test, termination, Bland’s rule
6. **Two-phase simplex** — auxiliary LP, phase I feasibility, phase II
7. **Geometry of simplex** — polyhedra, convexity, extreme points, BFS equivalence
8. **Duality** — dual construction, weak duality, strong duality
9. **Complementary slackness** — CS conditions, optimality geometry, Farkas’ lemma
10. **Integer programs** — integer hull, LP relaxation, cutting planes
11. **Convex optimization** — convex functions, subgradients, supporting halfspaces, Slater, KKT

Prerequisite edges are stored in `data/course-map.json`. The UI treats them as study guidance rather than hard locks.
